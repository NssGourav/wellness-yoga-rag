TITLE: Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing)
This breathing practice involves slow, controlled breathing through alternate nostrils.
It helps calm the nervous system and reduce stress.
Avoid if you have severe respiratory infections or nasal blockage.
Source: Summarized from Art of Living (https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday).
