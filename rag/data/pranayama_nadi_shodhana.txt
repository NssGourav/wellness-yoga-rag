TITLE: Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing)  
CATEGORY: Pranayama – Breathing Exercise – Beginner Level
DESCRIPTION: A breathing practice involving slow, controlled breathing through alternate nostrils.
BENEFITS:
  - Helps calm the nervous system and reduce stress.
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid if you have severe respiratory infections or nasal blockage.
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners and those with knee injuries. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
