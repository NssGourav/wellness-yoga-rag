TITLE: Paschim_Namaskarasana (Reverse Prayer Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Joining the palms together behind the back with fingertips pointing upward. 
BENEFITS:
  - Opens the chest and improves posture 
  - Targets the shoulders and quiets the mind  
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with shoulder injuries like rotator cuff issues 

WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - People seeking better posture. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
