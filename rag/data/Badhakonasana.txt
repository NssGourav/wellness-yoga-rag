TITLE: Badhakonasana (Butterfly Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Sitting Pose – Beginner Level   
DESCRIPTION:
  - A seated posture where the soles of the feet are brought together and knees move gently. 
BENEFITS:
  - Improves flexibility in the inner thighs and hips.
  - Stimulates abdominal organs to aid digestion.
  - Relieves fatigue from long hours of standing. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Practice gently or avoid with knee or groin injuries.
  - Do not force the knees to the floor. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners and those with hip stiffness.
