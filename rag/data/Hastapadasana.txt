TITLE: Hastapadasana (Standing Bend)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - A standing bend where the upper body folds toward the legs to make the spine more supple. 
BENEFITS:
  - Refreshing and invigorating for the nervous system 
  - Stretches all the muscles located along the back 
  - Improves spinal flexibility 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with chronic back or neck injuries 
  - Not recommended for those recovering from leg injuries 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners looking for back flexibility. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
