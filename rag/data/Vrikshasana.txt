TITLE: Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)
CATEGORY:
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - A balance where one foot is placed against the opposite inner thigh with arms overhead. 
BENEFITS:
  - Develops better stability and mental focus 
  - Strengthens legs and opens the hips 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with high blood pressure or vertigo 
  - Not for those with ankle or hip injuries 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners wanting to improve focus. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
