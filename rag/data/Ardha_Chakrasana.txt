TITLE: Ardha Chakrasana (Half Spinal Twist)
CATEGORY: 
Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Balancing on one hand while extending the other to create a gentle arc with the upper torso. 
BENEFITS:
  - Stretches the front of the body and chest 
  - Tones the muscles in the shoulders and arms  
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with wrist, arm, or shoulder injuries 
  - Skip if balance is a challenge due to medical conditions 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners wanting to open the chest. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
