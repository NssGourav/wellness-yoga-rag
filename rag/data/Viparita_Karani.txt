TITLE: Viparita_Karani (Legs Up The Wall Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Lying Pose (Back) – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Lying on the back with legs resting vertically against a wall. 
BENEFITS:
  - Restorative pose that refreshes the legs and feet.
  - Effective for calming the brain and reducing anxiety.
  - Improves circulation and reduces ankle swelling. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with serious eye conditions like glaucoma.
  - Use a pillow if you have chronic neck or back pain. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners and those standing all day. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
