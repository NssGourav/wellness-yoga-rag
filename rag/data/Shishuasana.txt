TITLE: Shishuasana (Child's Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Sitting Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Kneeling on the floor and folding the chest over the thighs in a resting position. 
BENEFITS:
  - Deeply relaxing stretch for the back and shoulders.
  - Calms the nervous system and quiets the mind.
  - Gently stretches the hips and tops of the ankles. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with serious knee injuries.
  - Keep the head slightly elevated if you have high blood pressure. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - People needing stress relief and beginners.
