TITLE: Kapalbhati Pranayama
CATEGORY: Pranayama – Breathing Exercise – Beginner Level
DESCRIPTION: A breathing technique involving forceful exhalations and passive inhalations.
BENEFITS:
  - Helps improve digestion and energize the body.
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Should be avoided during pregnancy or recent surgery.
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners and those with knee injuries. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
