The reverse prayer pose involves joining the palms together behind the back.
It is a great way to improve posture and open up the chest area.
This stretch targets the shoulders while helping to quiet the mind.
A towel can be held between the hands if they do not comfortably meet.
Individuals with shoulder injuries like rotator cuff issues should avoid this.
Ensure you move slowly into the position to avoid straining the joints.
