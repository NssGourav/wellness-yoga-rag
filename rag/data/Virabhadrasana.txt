TITLE: Virabhadrasana (Warrior Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - A strong lunge position with one leg forward and arms extended high overhead. 
BENEFITS:
  - Increases stamina and strengthens the arms 
  - Recommended for stiff or "frozen" shoulders 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with high blood pressure or heart issues 
  - Not for those with chronic injuries to the knees or back  
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Those in seated jobs and beginners. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
