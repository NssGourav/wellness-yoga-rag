TITLE: Dhanurasana (Bow Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Lying Pose (Stomach) – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Lying on the stomach and reaching back to hold the ankles, forming a bow shape. 
BENEFITS:
  - Stretches the front of the body and the throat.
  - Adds great flexibility to the back and strengthens legs.
  - Massages internal organs for better digestive health. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with high blood pressure or heart conditions.
  - Skip with migraines or lower back injuries. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners looking for a deep front stretch.
