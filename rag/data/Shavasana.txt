TITLE: Shavasana (Corpse Pose)
The corpse pose is a total relaxation position where you lie flat on your back.
It is used to integrate the benefits of a yoga session and calm the body.
This pose reduces stress and helps to lower the heart rate naturally.
It is a vital part of practice for restoring energy and mental clarity.
Place a small pillow under your knees if you experience lower back discomfort.
Try to keep the body perfectly still to achieve a deep state of rest.
