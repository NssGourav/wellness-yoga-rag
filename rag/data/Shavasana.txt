TITLE: Shavasana (Corpse Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Lying Pose (Back) – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - A total relaxation position where you lie flat and motionless on your back. 
BENEFITS:
  - Integrates the benefits of the yoga session.
  - Reduces stress and lowers the heart rate naturally.
  - Restores energy and mental clarity. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Use a pillow under knees for lower back discomfort.
  - Keep the body still for a deep state of rest. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: Everyone at the end of a session. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
