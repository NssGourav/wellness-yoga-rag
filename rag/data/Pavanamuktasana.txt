TITLE: Pavanamuktasana (Wind-Relieving Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Lying Pose (Back) – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Lying on the back and hugging the knees closely to the chest. 
BENEFITS:
  - Massages digestive organs and helps release trapped gas.
  - Loosens muscles in the lower back and hips.
  - Promotes a sense of physical ease. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with recent abdominal surgery or a hernia.
  - Keep head on the floor if you have neck injuries. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners with digestive discomfort.
