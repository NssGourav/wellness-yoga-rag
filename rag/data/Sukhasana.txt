TITLE: Sukhasana (Easy Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Sitting Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - A simple cross-legged sitting position used for centering and breathing. 
BENEFITS:
  - Strengthens the back and improves overall body posture.
  - Widely used as a foundation for seated meditation and relaxation.
  - Encourages a sense of mental calm and reduces physical tiredness. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - People with recent or chronic knee injuries should sit on a cushion for support.
  - Avoid sitting in this position for long periods if you experience lower back pain. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners and those with knee injuries.
