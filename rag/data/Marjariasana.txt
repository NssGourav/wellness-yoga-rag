TITLE: Marjariasana (Cat-Cow Pose)
CATEGORY: Yoga Asana – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: Moving the spine up and down while on hands and knees in a rhythmic motion. 
BENEFITS:
  - Brings mobility and warmth to the back muscles.
  - Improves blood circulation throughout the spinal column.
  - Releases physical stress in the shoulders. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Move slowly with chronic neck or wrist pain.
  - Do not over-arch if the lower spine is sensitive. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners and those with stiff backs.
