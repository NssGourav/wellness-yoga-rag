Konasana, also known as the sideways bending pose, is a simple standing yoga stretch.
In this posture, one arm is lifted upward while the body gently bends to the side.
This movement supports balance and improves body coordination.
It helps release tightness along the sides of the body and the spine.
Many people practice this pose to support comfortable digestion.
Individuals with neck or hip injuries should avoid this pose.
It is also not recommended for those experiencing high blood pressure or migraines.
