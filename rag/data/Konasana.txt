TITLE: Konasana (Sideways Bending Pose)
CATEGORY: 
 - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION:
  - Konasana is a standing sideways bending yoga posture. In this pose, one arm is raised upward while the torso gently bends to the side, stretching the lateral muscles of the body. 
BENEFITS:
  - Improves balance and body coordination  
  - Releases tension along the sides of the body and spine 
  - Increases flexibility of the back and hips 
  - Supports healthy digestion 
  - Helps reduce mild physical stiffness  
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid if you have neck, hip, or spinal injuries 
  - Not recommended for people with high blood pressure 
  - Avoid during migraines or severe dizziness 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners and those seeking light stretching.
