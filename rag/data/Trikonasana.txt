TITLE: Trikonasana (Triangle Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Stretching the legs wide and reaching down to create a triangular shape with the body. 
BENEFITS:
  - Improves digestion and physical stability 
  - Reduces stress and mild anxiety 
  - Helps relieve back pain  
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with recent injuries to the knees or hips 
  - Not recommended for those with heart problems or dizziness 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - People seeking mental and physical balance. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
