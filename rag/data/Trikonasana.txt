The triangle pose is created by spreading the legs and reaching one hand toward the ground.
It is widely practiced to help improve both physical and mental stability.
This position can help reduce feelings of stress and general anxiety.
Many find it useful for improving digestion and relieving minor back pain.
Avoid this pose if you are currently experiencing dizziness or heart problems.
Individuals with recent injuries to the knees or hips should skip this stretch.
