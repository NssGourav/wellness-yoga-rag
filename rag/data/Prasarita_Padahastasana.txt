TITLE: Prasarita_Padahastasana (Wide Legged Forward Bend)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Standing with feet wide apart while the torso bends forward toward the floor. 
BENEFITS: 
  - Grounding posture that stretches the hamstrings and lower back 
  - Helps calm the mind and find relaxation  
CONTRAINDICATIONS: 
  - Avoid during the third trimester of pregnancy 
  - Skip if you have acute lower back pain or knee injuries 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE:  
  - Beginners looking for relaxation. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
