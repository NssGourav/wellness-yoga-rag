TITLE: Utkatasana (Chair Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: Mimicking the action of sitting in an imaginary chair with the arms raised high. 
BENEFITS:
  - Strengthens the legs, back, and core muscles 
  - Builds inner willpower and physical balance  
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with recent injuries to the ankles or knees 
  - Skip if you have chronic lower back pain 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners building strength. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
