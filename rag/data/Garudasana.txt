The eagle pose requires wrapping the arms and legs together while balancing.
It is designed to enhance coordination and improve overall body stability.
The posture stretches the upper back, shoulders, and outer thighs.
It is an effective pose for developing deep focus and concentration.
You should avoid this pose if you have neck pain or lower back issues.
Individuals with knee injuries should not practice the interlacing leg movement.
