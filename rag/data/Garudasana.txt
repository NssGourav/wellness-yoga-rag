TITLE: Garudasana (Eagle Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Wrapping the arms and legs together while balancing on one foot. 
BENEFITS:
  - Enhances coordination and body stability 
  - Stretches the upper back, shoulders, and outer thighs 
  - Effective for developing deep concentration  
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with neck pain or lower back issues 
  - Not for individuals with knee injuries 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners needing coordination. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
