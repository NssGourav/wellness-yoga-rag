TITLE: Katichakrasana (Standing Spinal Twist)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Standing Pose – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - A standing posture involving a gentle rotation of the waist to stretch the hips and the entire length of the spine. 
BENEFITS  :
  - Relieves constipation and supports digestion 
  - Strengthens the neck and shoulders 
  - Beneficial for individuals with desk-bound jobs 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid if you have a hernia 
  - Not for individuals with spinal conditions or heart problems 
  - Skip if you have high blood pressure 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Office workers and beginners. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
