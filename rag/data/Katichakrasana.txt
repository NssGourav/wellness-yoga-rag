The standing spinal twist is a posture involving a gentle rotation of the waist.
This movement helps to stretch the hips and the entire length of the spine.
It is particularly useful for building strength in the neck and shoulders.
People with desk-bound jobs often find this pose helpful for relieving daily stiffness.
Individuals with a hernia or heart conditions should skip this practice.
Those with existing spinal injuries or high blood pressure should avoid this twist.
