TITLE: Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)
CATEGORY: 
  - Yoga Asana – Lying Pose (Stomach) – Beginner Level  
DESCRIPTION: 
  - Lying on the stomach and gently lifting the chest upward using the back muscles.  
BENEFITS:
  - Strengthens the entire length of the spine.
  - Opens the chest and improves upper body flexibility.
  - Tones the abdominal muscles. 
CONTRAINDICATIONS:
  - Avoid with a hernia or recent abdominal surgery.
  - Not recommended during pregnancy. 
WHO SHOULD PRACTICE: 
  - Beginners wanting to improve posture. 
SOURCE: Art of Living – https://www.artofliving.org/in-en/yoga/beginners/beginner-yoga-asanas-for-everyday
